Full Story

From The Family Table To La Casita

For more than two decades, Antolin Gamez built his life around the kitchen, learning what it takes to make honest food that people remember. Over those 23 years, the family recipes stayed close, shaped by tradition, patience, and the kind of cooking that asks you to slow down and do it right.

La Casita was born from the idea that Napoleon deserved a place where Mexican food felt real, generous, and deeply personal. Not a rushed version. Not a shortcut. A place where grilled meats, sauces, rice, beans, tortillas, and salsas are treated with care because they carry the flavor of home.

The restaurant reflects the values behind the recipes: family, consistency, and welcome. Every plate is meant to feel like it came from a kitchen that respects where it came from and who it is being served to.

Today, La Casita is more than a restaurant name. It is the Gamez family bringing their heritage to the table and inviting the community to be part of it, one meal at a time.
